Thanks to the GNOME Foundation, a handful of designers and developers got together last week in London to refocus on the core element of the GNOME experience, the shell. [Allan](https://blogs.gnome.org/aday/2017/11/22/gnome-shell-ux-hackfest/) and [Cassidy](https://medium.com/system76/gnome-ux-hackfest-2017-3b3db7298155) have already summed up everything in their well written blog posts, so I'd like to point to some pretty pictures and the video above.

Stay tuned for some higher fidelity proposals in the areas of app switching & launching and the lock/login experience.
